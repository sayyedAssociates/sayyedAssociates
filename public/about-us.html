Our Firm History
At SAYYED & ASSOCIATES, we are committed to delivering exceptional legal services with professionalism, integrity, and strategic expertise. As one of Mumbai’s most respected full-service law firms, we represent clients across a wide range of practice areas including criminal law, civil litigation, property and real estate matters, family and matrimonial disputes, motor accident claims, and more.

Our team of seasoned advocates is known for their courtroom excellence, deep legal knowledge, and dedication to client success. Whether you are seeking anticipatory or regular bail, contesting a divorce, dealing with a complex property dispute, or fighting for justice in a criminal case, our lawyers provide personalized legal solutions with clarity, confidence, and compassion.

With a strong presence before the Sessions Court, High Court, and Supreme Court of India, our litigation practice is the hallmark of our firm. We believe in preparing every case with meticulous attention to detail—factually and legally—while ensuring that our clients are informed and empowered throughout the legal process.

From NDPS cases to inheritance claims, from domestic violence matters to succession issues, our comprehensive legal services cover every stage from consultation and drafting to trial, appeal, and special leave petitions (SLPs). At SAYYED & ASSOCIATES, our goal is not just to represent you—it is to defend your rights, secure your interests, and stand by your side as legal allies.

If you are looking for the best criminal lawyer in Mumbai, or need expert advice in any civil or family-related matter, you’ve come to the right place. Reach out to us today to schedule a consultation and take the first step toward effective legal resolution.

Frequently Asked Questions
What areas of law do you specialize in?

We offer a wide range of legal services including civil litigation, criminal defense, family law, corporate law, property disputes, contract law, and more. Whether you need legal protection, counselling, or private legal representation, our experienced attorneys are here to help.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office directly or by filling out the contact form on our website. We offer both in-person and virtual appointments to suit your needs.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring any relevant documents related to your legal matter (contracts, court papers, identification, correspondence, etc.). The more information you provide, the better we can understand your situation and offer precise guidance.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. Client confidentiality is one of our top priorities. All communications and documents are kept strictly private in accordance with professional legal standards and ethics.

How much do your services cost?

Our fees vary depending on the type and complexity of the case. We are transparent about our billing practices and will discuss all fees and payment options with you during your initial consultation.

Do you offer legal advice without taking a case?

Yes. Our legal counselling services include one-time consultations where we provide advice, review documents, or help you understand your legal options—even if you don’t retain us for full representation.

We combine personalized attention with deep legal expertise. Our clients value our integrity, confidentiality, and proactive approach to solving legal issues. We take time to understand each client's unique situation and provide tailored solutions, not just standard responses.
